Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Peel the cucumber and wash in normal water along with the tomato
  • Cut the carrot, tomato and onion into rings.
  • Take mushroom and cut into small pieces.
  • Sprinkle some salt and chaat masala.
  • Serve this nutritional salad with any meal.
  • You can add your choice of vegetables such as onion,beed root, radish, etc.
A salad is a dish consisting of mixed, mostly natural ingredients with at least one raw ingredient. They are typically served at room temperature or chilled, though some can be served warm.
